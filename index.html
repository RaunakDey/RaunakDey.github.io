Raunak Dey

I am a fourth-year Physics Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Physics at the University of Maryland, College Park. I am advised by Prof. Joshua Weitz and mentored by Dr. Stephen Beckett. My research focuses on solving inverse problems and optimization problems with time series data. Given data points ordered in time, I aim to develop

  • interpretable models to infer the underlying phenomena governing the dynamics of the system;
  • optimal methods to perform a downstream task under constraints.
